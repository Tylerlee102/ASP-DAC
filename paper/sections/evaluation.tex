\subsection{Replay and Corruption Results}

Table~\ref{tab:replay-success} reports \rcFullReplayPass/\rcFullReplayTotal\ compiler-backed host-driven full RTL replay rows. Table~\ref{tab:negative} reports \rcNegativeReject\ corrupted-capsule rows rejected, \rcNegativeUnexpectedAccept\ unexpected accepts, and \rcNegativeNA\ not-applicable rows for corruption classes outside the current commit-index harness.

\subsection{Runtime and Mapping}

Table~\ref{tab:runtime} reports measured cycle, commit, and simulator wall-time summaries for \texttt{baseline\_no\_recorder}, \texttt{recorder\_present\_disabled}, and \texttt{recorder\_enabled}. Cycle and commit medians are separate from simulator wall-clock measurements.

Table~\ref{tab:mapped-overhead} reports same-target ECP5 place-and-route for the board-scoped baseline and ReplayCapsule designs. The full-core mapped rows use the same \rcMappedTarget\ target, the same Yosys synth\_ecp5 plus nextpnr-ecp5 flow, the same memory size, and the same board-level I/O assumptions. The recorder presence check passes on the mapped ReplayCapsule netlist.

\subsection{Expanded Scaling Package}

The expanded artifact adds workload scaling, capsule baseline comparisons, buffer-depth sensitivity, recorder-configuration sensitivity, runtime scaling, mapped memory/buffer/configuration scaling, event-sufficiency ablations, and mapped-failure diagnosis. These results are generated from \texttt{results/processed/workload\_scaling.csv}, \texttt{capsule\_baseline\_comparison.csv}, \texttt{buffer\_sensitivity.csv}, \texttt{runtime\_scaling.csv}, \texttt{mapped\_scaling.csv}, \texttt{mapped\_scaling\_overhead.csv}, \texttt{recorder\_config\_replay.csv}, and \texttt{event\_ablation\_scaling.csv}. Trace-baseline rows that are not directly emitted by the RTL are labeled as estimated in their CSV notes.

The mapped scaling data must be interpreted as FPGA recorder/instrumented-wrapper evidence. It does not claim ASIC area or power, and it does not implement an autonomous synthesizable replay-consume datapath. Simulator wall time is reported separately from architectural cycle and commit counts.
