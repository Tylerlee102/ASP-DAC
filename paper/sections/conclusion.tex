\section{Conclusion}

ReplayCapsule-RV shows that a scoped class of embedded RV32I interrupt/MMIO failures can be recorded as commit-indexed boundary-event capsules and replayed in an RTL harness with compiler-backed firmware. The artifact reports \rcFullReplayPass/\rcFullReplayTotal\ v1 full RTL replay rows, \rcVTwoReplayPass/\rcVTwoReplayTotal\ v2 rows with RTL capsule-source and MMIO/IRQ replay stimulus, replay-mode-controller captured-store self-replay handoff evidence, corruption rejection, expanded benchmark coverage, runtime summaries, buffer-depth tradeoffs, hard trace-size baselines, same-target FPGA overhead, and Nangate45 OpenROAD global-routed area/timing/power rows. The result is not a replacement for trace standards or snapshots; it is a hardware capsule design and evidence package for replay-driving boundary events. The next step is detailed ASIC signoff, standalone replay flow beyond Verilator reset/peripheral orchestration, and broader platform modeling, not stronger claims from the current prototype.
