\section{Conclusion}

ReplayCapsule-RV shows that a scoped class of embedded RV32I interrupt/MMIO failures can be recorded as commit-indexed boundary-event capsules and replayed in a host-driven RTL harness with compiler-backed firmware. The artifact reports \rcFullReplayPass/\rcFullReplayTotal\ full RTL replay PASS rows, corruption rejection, runtime summaries, buffer-depth tradeoffs, and same-target FPGA overhead. The result is not a replacement for trace standards or snapshots; it is a record-side hardware capsule design and evidence package for replay-driving boundary events. The next step is an integrated full-core replay-consume datapath and broader platform modeling, not stronger claims from the current prototype.
