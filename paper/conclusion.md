# Conclusion

ReplayCapsule-RV investigates a precise embedded-debug question: which hardware-visible events are sufficient to deterministically replay RV32I safety failures caused by interrupts and MMIO? The repository now contains the formal scaffold, bounded event-tap, event-classifier/slicer, property-checker, hash-signature, MMIO/interrupt logger, register, replay-control, replay-mismatch, recorder, and capsule-buffer proof/cover checks, synthesizable record-side RTL modules, a replay comparator, smoke experiments, twelve PicoRV32 wrapper firmware smokes, a seeded RTL-smoke interrupt reproducibility campaign, twelve failing/fixed RTL-smoke capsule export self-compare, missing-event, duplicate-event, metadata-corruption, payload-corruption, order-corruption, and PC-context checks, RTL/firmware-sim alignment rows, generic Yosys cell counts, and a no-fake-results evaluation pipeline. The next step is to scale the PicoRV32 smokes into a benchmark-wide RTL replay suite and collect RTL-backed replay, ablation, and mapped FPGA timing/resource results.
