# Conclusion

ReplayCapsule-RV investigates a precise embedded-debug question: which hardware-visible events are sufficient to deterministically replay RV32I safety failures caused by interrupts and MMIO? The repository now contains the formal scaffold, synthesizable record-side RTL modules, a replay comparator, smoke experiments, twelve PicoRV32 wrapper firmware smokes, generic Yosys cell counts, and a no-fake-results evaluation pipeline. The next step is to scale the PicoRV32 smokes into a benchmark-wide RTL replay suite and collect RTL-backed replay, ablation, and mapped FPGA timing/resource results.
