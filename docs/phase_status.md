## Scope and Evidence Rules

Only Phases 0, 1, and 2 are explicitly named in `docs/research_plan.md`.
Phases 3 through 10 below are derived from the documented workstreams in the
research plan, verification plan, baseline plan, ablation plan, evaluation
methodology, artifact notes, and paper scaffold. They should be treated as an
honest working matrix, not as a pre-existing canonical phase list.

Status vocabulary:

- Complete: phase artifacts are present at the intended current scope.
- Partially complete: source, docs, or model-level results exist, but the phase
  is not end-to-end complete.
- TODO: only plans or placeholders exist.
- Blocked-by-missing-tool: phase acceptance depends on a missing local tool.

Current local tool caveat:

  HDL tools from `.tools/oss-cad-suite/`, when installed outside git tracking.
- Generated CSVs and SVGs were regenerated by the local gate after the
  six-benchmark model expansion, Yosys synthesis pass, formal expansion, and

## Bottom Line

Phases 0 through 2 have a credible scaffold, static RTL evidence, model-level
evidence, firmware-sim evidence, RTL-smoke capsule exports, twelve PicoRV32 wrapper smokes, bounded SMTBMC recorder and capsule-buffer checks, and generic Yosys
synthesis evidence, but they should not be described as completed benchmark-wide
hardware replay. Phases 3 through 9 are partially built at the
documentation/model/firmware-sim/RTL-smoke/pipeline/synthesis level. The
hardware-dependent parts of Phase 10 are still blocked by missing local
full-simulation build support, compiler, and mapped FPGA tools.
